Students Organization

It was a job where I learned about the dynamics and the process of creating a large event from scratch. I was responsible for co-creating creative concepts of our program and music line-up, contacting the sponsors, guest service, issuing media accreditations and taking care of artists. My role didn’t end with the organization, I also worked during the whole event, caring for the continuity of the event. All this helped me develop my organizational and service skills, my ability to work in a team and the effectiveness of communication. The greatest value of this experience was the satisfaction of the event participants. Thanks to the fact that I participated in the preparation and implementation process, I had direct contact with our recipients as well. I could see the effects of my hard work with my own eyes, how the ideas were implemented and how our guests reacted, which gave me a huge satisfaction.

Theater internship

During the internship I was responsible for developing a concept for the promotion of a theatrical performance. To create it I used a design thinking methodology, which allowed me to understand the essence of this project and create a completely different and unforgettable experience for the viewers. Design thinking is an extremely useful and universal approach that changes the way you look at a project, puts the recipient at the center of all your activities, no matter what you do. It was a perfect example that culture and creativity can go hand in hand with thinking about business and the needs of the recipients. I was very satisfied with the fact that all the ideas and the whole concept that I developed during the internship was realized. Starting with the forms of reaching and communicating with recipients to the visual concept of invitations, gadgets and planning the theater space. As a result, I had a real impact on the creation of promotional communication in every aspect.

Polish Film Festival in Gdynia

During the two editions of this festival, I was able to practice my skills in many tasks related to the organization of the event. The direct contact with Polish and foreign guests was a valuable value of this experience. I was helping guide them through intricate booking systems, providing information and advising about the program. I took care of the comfort and satisfaction with participation. The work required quick reactions, good time management, and the right approach and excellent service. I have often worked under stressful conditions in which I had to be professional and mindful. I realized that I was part of a prestigious event and I had a real influence on its quality and success. What is important to me, it was a combination of work with passion and a perfect opportunity to work with inspiring people, motivated to act like me.
